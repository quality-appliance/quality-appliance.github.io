Installment Application Guide

  1. 1

    Register & Login

    Create your account by clicking Register. Already have an account? Login to access your dashboard.

  2. 2

    Access the Application Portal

    Navigate to the Application Portal from the main menu. Here, you can start your installment application process.

  3. 3

    Fill Out the Application Form

    Complete the online application form with your personal, employment, and financial details. Make sure all information is accurate to avoid delays.

  4. 4

    Submit Required Documents

    Upload clear copies of required documents such as valid ID, proof of income, proof of billing, and other supporting documents as specified in the form.

  5. 5

    Wait for Application Review

    Our team will review your application and documents. You will be notified via email or SMS regarding the status of your application, or if additional information is needed.

  6. 6

    Approval & Down Payment

    Once approved, you will receive your installment plan details. Pay the required down payment at your chosen branch or via online payment options.

  7. 7

    Pick Up or Delivery

    After down payment, schedule a pick-up at your preferred store or arrange for home delivery of your appliance.

  8. 8

    Monthly Installment Payments

    Pay your monthly dues on time through our various payment channels (in-store, online, or partner payment centers) to avoid penalties and keep your account in good standing.

  9. 9

    Completion & Ownership

    Once all payments are completed, you will receive a Certificate of Full Payment and full ownership of your appliance.
