Installment Application Guide

Follow these steps before applying

  1. 1

    Register & Login

    Create an account via Register. Already registered? Login.

  2. 2

    Access the Application Portal

    Navigate to the Application Portal from the main menu.

  3. 3

    Fill Out the Application Form

    Provide your personal, employment, and financial details accurately.

  4. 4

    Submit Required Documents

    Upload valid ID, proof of income, billing, and other supporting documents.

  5. 5

    Wait for Application Review

    Our team will notify you via email/SMS about your application status.

  6. 6

    Approval & Down Payment

    Upon approval, pay the down payment online or in-store.

  7. 7

    Pick Up or Delivery

    Schedule pickup or delivery of your appliance post down payment.

  8. 8

    Monthly Installment Payments

    Pay on time via store, online, or payment partners to avoid penalties.

  9. 9

    Completion & Ownership

    After your last payment, receive a Certificate of Full Payment and full ownership.
