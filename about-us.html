Our Story

Quality Appliance Group is a dealer of appliances, consumer electronics and furniture based in the Philippines.

Incorporated on December 1, 1988, Quality Appliance Group opened its first store in Cagayan de Oro City on May 19, 1989.

Through the years, the company has been at the forefront of bringing to market the latest home and business solutions from the world's leading brands and making them accessible through various payment methods.

The company offers flexible payment options including cash, bank card, digital, third-party consumer financing and in-house installment plans.

At the heart of the business of Quality Appliance Group is its strong partnership with its Salesforce.

A group of dynamic, diversified and dedicated individuals that promote the products and services of the company beyond the four corners of its physical stores.

Through this relationship, Quality Appliance Group was able to provide opportunities to thousands of people in the communities it serves, turning ordinary citizens into entrepreneurs and uplifting their lives for the better.

Under the umbrella of Quality Appliance Group are five corporations.

Quality Appliance Plaza, Inc, Oro Holiday Appliance Plaza, Inc, Special Appliance Plaza, Inc, Rainbow Appliance Plaza, Inc and Central Quality Appliance, Inc.

These five corporations contribute a total of thirty-eight (38) retail outlets across the Philippines, including CQ Lifestyle Center, a premium lifestyle solutions store, and a franchise of Beyond The Box, an Apple Premium Reseller.
