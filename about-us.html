About Our Company

Quality Appliance Plaza

Since 1988, Quality Appliance Plaza has been a trusted name in the Philippines for appliances, consumer electronics, and furniture. We empower homes and businesses with the latest solutions from the world's top brands—made accessible through flexible payment options and exceptional service.

Our Journey

  1. 1988

    Founded

    Quality Appliance Plaza was established in Cagayan de Oro, aiming to provide affordable, high-quality appliances to Filipino households.

  2. 1998

    First Major Expansion

    Opened additional branches in Mindanao, bringing trusted brands closer to more communities.

  3. 2008

    Top Dealer Recognition

    Recognized among the top 10 dealers for major brands such as Sharp, Sony, and Carrier in the Philippines.

  4. 2016

    Customer Service Innovation

    Launched in-house installment plans and enhanced after-sales support, making appliances more accessible and service more reliable.

  5. 2026

    E-Commerce Launch

    Launched our online platform, making it easier for customers nationwide to shop for appliances and home solutions.

Our Mission

To enrich Filipino lives by providing quality appliances, furniture, and lifestyle solutions. We are committed to delivering outstanding customer service, a diverse product selection, and flexible payment options—making premium products accessible to every Filipino home.

Our Vision

To transform Filipino households through innovative home solutions. We strive to be the most trusted retail partner nationwide, maintain our standing among the top dealers for major brands, and continually elevate the shopping experience through technology and customer-centric services.
